Reading Candlestick Patterns

Master the language of the market. Candlesticks show you the battle between Bulls (buyers) and Bears (sellers).

The Bullish Hammer

A Hammer occurs at the bottom of a downtrend and signals a potential reversal. It has a small body and a long lower wick.
