// 02 PRICE ACTION PATTERNS

Support & Resistance

Support is the price level where a downtrend tends to pause due to a concentration of demand. Resistance is where an uptrend pauses due to a concentration of supply.

Trendlines

Drawing trendlines helps identify the current direction of the market. An uptrend is defined by Higher Highs and Higher Lows.

// 03 TECHNICAL INDICATORS

Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD)

The MACD is a trend-following momentum indicator that shows the relationship between two moving averages of an asset’s price. The "MACD line" is calculated by subtracting the 26-period EMA from the 12-period EMA.

How to Trade it:

  • • Bullish Crossover: MACD line crosses above the Signal line.
  • • Bearish Crossover: MACD line crosses below the Signal line.
