// 01 Market Basics

Price Action Kya Hai?

Price Action means price ki movement ko dekh kar trade lena. Hum indicators se pehle candles ko dekhte hain because Price is God (Bhav Bhagwan Che). Indicators sirf confirmation ke liye hote hain.

// 02 Chart Patterns

Double Bottom (W Pattern)

Yeh ek bullish pattern hai. Jab price ek hi level se do baar bounce kare, toh hum buy karte hain.

Head & Shoulders

Yeh bearish reversal hai. Jab price teen peaks banaye aur beech wali sabse badi ho.

// 03 Indicators

RSI (Relative Strength Index)

Kaise kaam karta hai? Simple: RSI 0-100 ke beech ghumta hai.

  • Overbought (70+): Price bohot upar ja chuka hai, sell karne ka time hai.
  • Oversold (30-): Price sasta mil raha hai, buy karne ka time hai.

Support & Resistance

Market kabhi bhi seedha upar nahi jata.

"Support is the floor, Resistance is the ceiling."
