Institutional Strategy

Smart Money Concepts (SMC)

SMC is based on tracking the activity of "Whales" (Banks, Institutions). These entities don't buy like retail traders; they leave traces in the form of **Order Blocks** and **Liquidity Sweeps**.

BOS (Break of Structure)

When the market breaks a significant swing high or low, confirming the continuation of a trend.

CHoCH (Change of Character)

The first sign of a trend reversal. Occurs when price breaks the opposite side of the structure.

Supply & Demand

Order Blocks (OB) Deep-Dive

An Order Block is a candle where large institutions placed their heavy orders. We wait for price to return to this "Block" before entering.

Bullish OB: The last down-candle before a massive impulsive move up.
Bearish OB: The last up-candle before a massive impulsive move down.
